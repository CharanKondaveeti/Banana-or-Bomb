How To Play

These are three main game indicators that you will enounter throughout the game

cash available Bet Amount Current winnings

How to start ‚ùì

Banana or Bomb Game starts by asking you to place the bet, Its mandatory to start the game.

Soon you fix the bet, the bet amount container blinks orange, that indicates bet is placed successfully. The amount will be be deducted from the cash available ( default 1500 ). Now the game starts

How game works ‚ùì

There are 7 level layers containing five wooden plates in each, the active layer is highlighted with the dark green background. One has to guess under which wooden plate the banana is hidden

If banana is guessed correctly, youll be taken to level 2 layer and as you keep guessing correctly the difficulty in guessing increases

if guessed is bomb, The game is over

How to win ‚ùì

As you keep guessing banana, Your winnings increase as you pass the levels.

  • layer1 -> Bet Amount * 1.5
  • layer2 -> Bet Amount * 2
  • layer3 -> Bet Amount * 2.5
  • layer4 -> Bet Amount * 4
  • layer5 -> Bet Amount * 6
  • layer6 -> Bet Amount * 10
  • layer7 -> Bet Amount * 17

If you are not sure about your next move, you can take your current winnings by clicking red dot. your winnings will be added to the your balance

current winnings container blinks orange on current winnings increment

if you go all through the level 7 layer, there youll win 17 times of your bet amount
